About

Hi, I'm Margarita.

Chemist. Marketer. Business Consultant. Education Reformer. Entrepreneur. Bilingual. Nerd. Wife. Mom. Animal Lover Extraordinaire!

As you can see, I've worn a lot of hats throughout my life. And because of that, I have a lot to offer.

I'm originally from Cuba, grew up in New Jersey, and now live in the Rockies. I worked in marketing for an international chemical company for 15 years, started and operated the first charter school in the state of Wyoming, and am now trying my hand at web development. I would love to work with you!

When I'm not working I love to travel, eat fabulous food, read interesting books, spend time with my family, and take walks with the best dog on the planet!
